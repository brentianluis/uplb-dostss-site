The Organization


The spirit of scholarship reigns in those who seek rational knowledge and drive it into action for the better and the common good. Since the beginning, the UPLB Department of Science and Technology Scholars’ Society (SS) has envisioned a society of boundless thinkers that seek genuine empowerment.

The organization believes in the advancement of the country through the promotion of science and technology. We are bounded by our goals. We are inspired by our existence.

Our Goal

Mission

The mission of the organization is to contribute to the Philippine Society through the promotion of Science and Technology, to encourage academic excellence among its members and to instill our ideals of equality, service, leadership and discipline.

Vision

The organization seeks to promote intellectual, social, cultural, physical and moral development among its members.

Our History

How It Began

In 1985, the National Science and Technology Authority (NSTA) scholars convened after realizing the need for an organization that will embody their ideals of maintaining academic excellence. Several meetings were held to discuss the plan regarding the establishment of a formal organization with its own constitution and set of by-laws. Soon after the ratification of the constitution, the UPLB NSTA Scholars’ Society was recognized as an academic organization on November 25, 1985.

The first president of NSTA SS was Meinrado F. Samala. All scholars of NSTA were recognized as members of the newly formed society. They were active in participating in various quiz contests, which won them several awards. But this dynamism lasted only for a year.

The Revival

In 1987, some of the charter members headed by Lope Doromal decided to revive the organization. This time it was named UPLB DOST Scholars’ Society (UPLB DOST SS) under the new leadership of Anna Marie V. Magbanua.

In 2013, the Philippine Alliance of DOST Scholars (PhilADS) was established having UPLB DOST SS representing the University of the Philippines Los Baños. Other founding organizations are from the University of the Philippines Diliman, Philippine Normal University, Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Rizal Technological University, and Technological University of the Philippines.

To date, UPLB DOST SS has 49 resident members who pull their talents together in sponsoring activities that promote better understanding of science and technology in the spirit of service.

Our Events

In line with its mandate to serve, lead, and excel, the UPLB DOST Scholars' Society conducts events which aim to provide selfless service to the Filipino people, hone skills of its members and the youth to lead, and promote an excellent Philippines for its betterment and for the generations to come.

Scholars' Week

This week-long celebration aims to celebrate the spirit of scholarship as the organization will hold several activities to provide service to fellow DOST scholars and welcome fellow UPLB students as the semester starts.

Blunders, Breakthroughs, Bahala Na Quiz Contest

As an avenue to celebrate the wonders of Science and Technology in the University, the organization conducts an annual quiz contest in relation to S&T, also as an opportunity of its promotion.

SIKAT

The official newsletter of the UPLB Department of Science and Technology Scholars’ Society. It features the highlights of the organization’s unending desire and passion to serve, lead, and excel.
